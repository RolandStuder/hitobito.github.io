hitobito is flexibly customizable to your organizational structure, so that even very complex structures and permissions can be decipted. As a web application, accessability from anywhere is granted, even with your smartphone.

Thanks to the central data storage, there are no redundancies. We have placed great emphasis on an intuitive and easy operability during the development. So even occasional users will find hitobito easy to master.

How we support you

  • Customizing hitobito to your individual needs
  • Developing the solution according to your wishes
  • Hosting incl. backup, support and maintenance
  • Taking care of the customer community and the community solution

Open Source

The source code of hitobito is published on Github under the GNU Affero General Public License. All software components (Ruby on Rails, MySQL database, ...) are open source and base on open standards and interfaces.
